## Why Quizymode

The name says it — **quiz + easy mode**. Making it simple to find a topic, review it, and actually learn from it.

And like Quasimodo of Notre-Dame, this app is built to be strong, reliable, and always there — quietly carrying a lot of knowledge, ready whenever you need it.

## What it does

Quizymode is a place to study, review, and quiz yourself using public question banks and your own personal collections.

Content lives in clear category and keyword paths so it is easy to browse, drill down, and return to later. Instead of one large undifferentiated pool of questions, material is organized into specific topics and subtopics — and anything that does not fit neatly can go into broader buckets like General Knowledge or Trivia.

## Use cases

- **Certification exams** — AWS, Azure, GCP, Kubernetes, PMP, and other professional certs
- **Standardized tests** — ACT, SAT, GRE, GMAT, NCLEX, and similar
- **School subjects** — biology, algebra, history, geography, chemistry, and more
- **Language learning** — English idioms, French vocabulary, Spanish grammar
- **Professional knowledge** — compliance, medical, legal, or industry-specific topics
- **Fun and trivia** — road trips, pub quizzes, family game nights, and general knowledge

## Creating your own content

You can upload questions, group them into collections, and share them with others. A teacher or tutor can upload a study guide, turn it into a collection, and share it with a class so students can review questions, see answers and explanations, and practice in quiz mode.

Content can be submitted for public review, and you can request more questions for any topic that needs better coverage.
