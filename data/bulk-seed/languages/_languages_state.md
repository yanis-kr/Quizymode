## Wording and style rules learned

- Use very plain English in stems and explanations.
- Prefer short, useful phrases and high-frequency vocabulary.
- Keep one clean learning point per item.
- Include stable English translation keywords for foreign-language word or phrase items.
- Favor beginner usefulness over rare exceptions or literary register.

## Weak patterns to avoid

- ambiguous translation traps
- grammar pedantry without practical value
- obscure exceptions too early in coverage
- jokey distractors that make the answer obvious
- region-specific usage presented as universal without a note
